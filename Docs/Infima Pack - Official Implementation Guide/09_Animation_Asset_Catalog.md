## Using this catalog

- Most setups mainly use the first-person character animations together with matching first-person weapon animations — check those two sections first.
- Every animation here maps to a field on `BP_TFA_BaseConfig` (see [02_Core_Data_Asset_BaseConfig.md](02_Core_Data_Asset_BaseConfig.md)) or a montage referenced from one of the AnimBPs — this catalog is what you're populating those fields *with*.
- The `Melee`, `Malfunctions`, `Interactions`, and `Healing` arrays on `BP_TFA_BaseConfig` pull from the Combat/Interactions categories above — group the relevant assets into those arrays rather than assigning them to single-asset fields.
